How To Reduce Picture Free

Follow these simple steps to reduce the size of your pictures for free:

  1. Click the "Choose File" button to upload your picture.
  2. Enter the percentage by which you want to reduce the picture size in the "Percent of Size" field.
  3. Click the "Process Now" button to resize your picture.
  4. Once processed, you can download the resized image by clicking the "Download Resized Image" button.
